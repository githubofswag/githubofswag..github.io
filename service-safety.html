Life Safety Training

When your employees, tenants and business partners feel safe, they can concentrate on being creative, productive, and successful.

High-value and mission critical assets need special protection. Irreplaceable cultural artifacts, medical facilities, power and energy storage equipment, and data centers often have very specific needs.

Iidon will help identify and train employees, tenants, and business partners in Fire and Life Safety as well as First Aid/CPR/AED training.

iidon has an intimate knowledge of your systems, procedures, and business culture. A robust life safety training program will provide building tenants, employees, and management staff with critical emergency preparedness information that could save their lives.
