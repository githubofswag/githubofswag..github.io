Risk Assessment/Consulting

Security Assessments are an excellent way to evaluate your existing security program and a great first step to take prior to making security improvements at your facility or when trying to solve a specific security problem.

During a Risk Assessment, all aspects of your security program are examined, any weaknesses are identified, and suggestions for security improvements are made. In addition, opportunities where costs can be reduced or where security operations can be made more efficient are identified.

Iidon Security consultants are highly trained subject matter experts in all areas of physical security design and implementation. Our staff have a demonstrated knowledge in threat assessment and risk analysis, physical security systems, and the appropriate identification, implementation, and ongoing evaluation of security measures. These are a few of the areas of inspection you can expect:

  • Physical Security
  • Access Control
  • Perimeter Protection
  • Backup Systems
  • Incident Reporting and Analysis
  • Employee and Contractor Training and Security Awareness
  • Emergency Response and Crisis Management
  • Hiring and Employment Termination Practices
  • Workplace Violence Prevention and Response
