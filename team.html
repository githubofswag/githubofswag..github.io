Marty Frye

Marty has over 28 years of experience in the commercial high rise real estate market in Dallas / Ft. Worth and Texas. He has serves the real estate community in positions ranging from Operations Manager, Branch Manager and Texas District Manager. He maintains countless strong relationships and is very well respected by clients from across the United States. He is currently an active member in BOMA, Downtown Security Directors Association, Infraguard (Homeland Security sponsored) and ASIS to name a few of his memberships. Overall, Marty brings a wealth of knowledge to the security industry and to iidon Security Associates.

Andy Easterling

Andy Easterling has over 28 years of experience in class-A high rise security. During his career he has served in the various capacities including director of security, quality assurance manager and district manager. Andy is an active member of ASIS, Dallas Downtown Emergency Response Team, Downtown Security Directors Association and has been a law enforcement official with a local police department since 1996. Andy joined iidon in 2005 as a senior operations manager and has remained an asset to our operations.

Larry Spencer

Larry Spencer has over 15 years of experience in the security and correctional / law enforcement industry. He began his career in the United States Army and proudly served our country for 15 years in the 82nd Airborne Division. Larry has been with iidon Security Associates since 2002. Larry has held various positions while at iidon that include Account Manager, Field Supervisor and Operations Manager.

Larry is passionate about customer service, client retention and ensuring that our employees are delivered first class service. Larry continues to be a vital component of the iidon operations team.

Greg Autry

Greg has over 30 years’ experience in the security industry. He began his career with the United States Air Force as a Security Policeman securing nuclear and aircraft resources serving both on active duty and in the reserves. In the private sector, Greg has filled several roles including Security Director, Regional Security Manager and Life Safety Manager. Greg obtained the Certified Protection Professional (CPP) through ASIS International in 2005. He is active in the Downtown Security Director’s Association and the Fort Worth Security Director’s group.
