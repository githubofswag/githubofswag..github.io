Our Training

Every successful organization prepares its team for optimal performance. Nonetheless, training has always been a paramount concern in our industry. At iidon, our training programs are designed with the success of your organization in mind.

CPR, First Aid & AED

• Every iidon associate receives CPR, First Aid and AED Training before arriving at your site.
• 100% classroom training means students are with an AHA Instructor for their entire
   learning experience.
• Instructor-led, hands-on class format reinforces skills proficiency, including:
  • • General Principles for notification during medical emergencies
  • • Recognition and emergency medical attention for stroke, heart attack and shock
  • • Administering electric shock to a victim using an AED (Automated External
       Defibrillator)
  • • Proper documentation of emergency incidents
  • • Proper use of safety equipment

PEER

iidon Associates receive regular propriety PEER (Proficient Execution and Emergency Response) Training. Our program ensures familiarity with your building’s specific protocols and resources, including:
  • • Bomb threats
  • • Access controls
  • • Elevator entrapments
  • • Water leaks
  • • Our role as service ambassadors
  • • Inclement weather
  • • Disturbances/Suspicious persons
  • • Customer service
  • • Fire alarms
  • • Medical emergencies
  • • Safety awareness
  • • Patrols
  • • Power outages
  • • Broken windows

iiRise

Our propriety iiRise customer service training combines best practices of Disney and Ritz Carlton to revolutionize the interaction between security, tenants and guests.
  • • Proper telephone techniques
  • • Handling difficult customers
  • • Defusing hostile situations
  • • Eye contact with a smile
  • • Our role as service ambassadors
  • • Everyone is a customer
  • • The value of courtesy and respect
  • • Proper email techniques
  • • Body language
  • • Viewing problems as service opportunities
  • • Being on post is like being on stage
  • • Offering options
  • • Choosing the right words to promote positive outcomes

Critical Threat Manual

Designed to better identify, report and respond tocritical situations. This manual serves as a supplement to existing training on subjects such as:
  • • Hazardous Materials
  • • Weapons of Mass Destruction
  • • Active Shooter
  • • Terrorism Awareness
  • • Suspicious Mail and Correspondence
  • • Cyber Security
  • • Evacuating High Rise Buildings
  • • Workplace Violence
