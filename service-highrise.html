High Rise

Our team is encouraged to become an extension of your management team.

Caring for your Tenants

iidon understands the relationships between you, your tenants and your prospective tenants. Whether they are a small single office or a full floor tenant, each requires the same professional attention with the same sense of urgency.


Extension of Your Management Team

The security personnel in your lobbies and patrolling your buildings represent your management team. We will ensure each associate impresses your tenants and visitors with their image, words, and deeds.


Technical Skills to Better Assist You

Whether we maintain the card access system or assist the engineering department with fire drills and monitoring mechanical systems, iidon can have the right personnel with the proper skill set to assist with these areas of need.


Your security team must be prepared to confidently handle:

  • Elevator emergencies
  • Inclement Weather evacuations
  • Fire and bomb threat evacuations
  • Medical Emergencies including administering CPR and using the AED
  • Evacuating handicapped persons from the tower
  • Airborne threats through your HVAC system
  • Securing emergency elevators

Each of our Associates are well trained for each of these situations. We make certain that we provide the peace of mind your property deserves.
